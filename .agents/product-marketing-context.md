# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-20*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Gallerify lets photographers build stunning client galleries and a public portfolio page in minutes.
**What it does:** Photographers create organized, fast-loading galleries with nested collections, share them with clients via a branded subdomain (yourname.gallerify.com), and control exactly when each gallery goes live. It replaces manual delivery methods (WeTransfer, Google Drive) with a professional, purpose-built experience.
**Product category:** Photography portfolio & client gallery software
**Product type:** SaaS (subscription)
**Business model:** Freemium (14-day trial, no card) → monthly subscription. Pro $7.99/mo, Pro Max $15.99/mo. Indonesia pricing: Pro $5.99/mo, Pro Max $11.99/mo. Payments via LemonSqueezy.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Photographers spend hours on clunky workarounds — Google Drive folders, WeTransfer links, emailed ZIPs — that feel unprofessional and frustrate clients.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Google Drive / Dropbox: Not designed for gallery viewing; no portfolio page; no branding
- Squarespace / Wix portfolios: No client-specific galleries; no privacy controls per gallery
- Pixieset / ShootProof: Established but can feel heavy or expensive for new photographers
**What it costs them:** Lost bookings from a weak first impression; hours of manual file management; client complaints about slow download experiences
**Emotional tension:** Embarrassment sending a Drive link after charging premium prices; anxiety over whether clients saw their work

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Pixieset, ShootProof, Pic-Time — full-featured but complex/expensive; overkill for solo photographers
**Secondary:** Squarespace / Format / Adobe Portfolio — portfolio sites without client-delivery functionality
**Indirect:** Google Drive, WeTransfer, Dropbox — free but completely unprofessional for client delivery

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Sub-1-second gallery load times (built for speed from the ground up)
- One platform: client galleries AND public portfolio page, on a branded subdomain
- Clean, minimal UI — no bloat, no confusing features photographers don't need
- Privacy controls: draft until you're ready; your timing, your rules
**How we do it differently:** Laser focus on the photographer's workflow — create, organize, share, impress — without the enterprise complexity of competitors
**Why that's better:** Less time in software, more time shooting
**Why customers choose us:** Speed + simplicity + professional presentation at a price that doesn't hurt early-career photographers

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I already use Google Drive" | Drive wasn't built for this — clients see a folder, not a gallery. Gallerify makes your work look as good as it actually is. |
| "Is 10 GB enough?" | 10 GB holds ~3,000–5,000 full-res JPEGs. Most photographers upgrade when they need it, not before. |
| "I'm not sure I need this yet" | Start free for 14 days — no card, no commitment. See how your clients react. |

**Anti-persona:** Hobbyist photographers who don't deliver to paying clients; photographers already locked into Pixieset with no desire to migrate

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Embarrassment using Drive/WeTransfer for paid clients; clients saying "where do I find my photos?"
**Pull:** A portfolio page that gets them bookings; galleries clients actually love opening
**Habit:** "I've always just sent a Drive link, it works fine"
**Anxiety:** Will it be hard to migrate my existing galleries? Will my clients be confused by a new link?
